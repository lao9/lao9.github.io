Goals for Mod 2

In Mod 1, I deliberately held back on my participation with the Turing community. In the past, my type-A-ness would get the best of me. I’d be involved in too many things and not get enough out of any of it. I’d just be tired, and all the extracurriculars took away from me actually learning and excelling in class.

Last mod, I was semi-involved with the Joan Clarke society, but hope to actually join a posse this mod and also work on a side project or two! I’d also like to lock down a mentor, too.
